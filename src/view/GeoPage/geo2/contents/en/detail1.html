Miao is an ancient nation, spread all over the world, mainly distributed in China's guizhou, hunan, hubei, sichuan, yunnan, guangxi, hainan and other provinces and regions, as well as southeast Asia, Laos, Vietnam, Thailand and other countries and regions.
Based on historical documents and miao word-of-mouth information, miao ancestors first live in the middle and lower reaches of the Yellow River region, its ancestor is the human-god, "three miao" period, migration to the jianghan plain, and then by reason of war, migration to the south, and west, gradually into the southwest mountainous area and the yunnan-guizhou plateau. Since the Ming and qing dynasties, some of the hmong migrated to southeast Asian countries, and in modern times they migrated from these places to the United States and Europe.
Miao nationality has its own language, the miao language is the han Tibetan language group miao dialect, which is divided into three dialects: xiangxi, qian dong and sichuan guizhou. Because of the long-term relationship between the miao and the han ethnic groups, some hmong and tongchinese are also used in Chinese. Miao's religious beliefs are mainly natural worship and ancestor worship.
